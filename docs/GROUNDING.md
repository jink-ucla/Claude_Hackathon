## 2. Exact thresholds (top of `build_dataset.py`, reweightable)

| Constant | Value | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| `STRONG_CATEGORY` | `">10 DE genes"` | `n_total_genes_category` tier ⇒ strong regulator (score +2) |
| `KD_FRAC_MIN` | 0.5 | ≥ half a gene's guides show significant knockdown |
| `DONOR_CORR_MIN` | 0.4 | cross-donor DE z-score correlation (mean over donor pairs) |
| `GUIDE_CORR_MIN` | 0.4 | cross-guide DE z-score correlation |
| `COEF_RANK_MIN` | 0.9 | polarization/aging regulator `coef_rank` tier (or `known_regulators`) |
| `AUTOIMMUNE_OR_MIN` / `AUTOIMMUNE_FDR_MAX` | 1.0 / 0.10 | cluster autoimmune odds ratio / BH-FDR |
| `CD4_SPECIFIC_R_MAX` | 0.2 | K562-vs-CD4 logFC r below this ⇒ CD4-specific |

Thresholds chosen from the shipped distributions: strong = the paper's own top DE-count bin
(7,570 rows); reproducibility cutoffs near the median of *measured* correlations (donor median
0.41, guide median 0.50); `coef_rank ≥ 0.9` = top-decile regulator.

- **33,983** perturbation × condition rows; **11,526** unique perturbed genes.
- On-target-significant: **6,869 / 7,154 / 7,193** (Rest / Stim8hr / Stim48hr).
- Convergent-score: **1,744** rows ≥ 6, **530** ≥ 7, **2** = 9 (BATF, BAHD1; both Stim8hr).
- Broadest Stim8hr trans-effect hubs: TADA2B, **CD3E (5,710), LAT (5,535), PLCG1 (5,032),
  ZAP70 (5,021)** — proximal TCR signaling.
- Autoimmune-enriched modules (OR>1 & FDR<0.1): **13 (Stim8hr) / 9 (Stim48hr) / 1 (Rest)** — most
  numerous at Stim8hr, closely followed by Stim48hr. (Membership-weighted this is 146 gene ×

  the row-gap reflects module size, not concentration.) Top modules: cluster 89 (OR 3.0, FDR 5×10⁻⁶), 59, 54.

## 4. Known-regulator recovery (built-in benchmark)

Max `regulator_score` across conditions: BATF 9 · STAT3 8 · STAT6 8 · GATA3 8 · RASA2 8 ·
CBLB 7 · TBX21 6 · VAV1 6 · MAP4K1 6 · BCL6 5 · IL2RA 4 · FOXP3 2 · STAT4 2 · RORC 1. Strong
recovery of activation and Th1/Th2/Th17-cytokine regulators; low recovery of the Treg/Th17
lineage-commitment TFs (FOXP3, RORC) is expected under these culture conditions — see §5.

## 5. Known limitations / honesty

- **Preprint**, not peer-reviewed; "novel" is the authors' framing.
- `DE_stats.suppl_table.csv` is a reduced summary; reproducibility columns come from the repo's
  dedicated correlation tables and cover only perturbations with ≥2 guides / ≥2 donors —
  NaN = "not measured here," not "not reproducible."
- FOXP3/RORC low scores reflect culture-condition coverage, not biological irrelevance.
- Arrayed validation covers only IL10/IL21 (13 perturbations) — a spot-check.
- `regulator_score` is a transparent ranking heuristic, not a statistical model.
- scLDM.CD4 (the dataset's companion generative model) is GPU-only and unbenchmarked; not used
  to produce any number here.
